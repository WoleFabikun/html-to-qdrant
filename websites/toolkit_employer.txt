Employer and HR Professional Toolkit
All the tools you'll need to help employees navigate their paid family and medical leave benefits.
We're Here For Your Benefit
Now more than ever, employees need time to give and receive care. NJ Paid Family and Medical Leave, also known as Temporary Disability and Family Leave Insurance, supports your employees and keeps your business healthy too.
We're Partners in Providing Benefit Information
Employees often first learn about Temporary Disability and Family Leave Insurance benefits from their employer. That’s why it’s essential for you to have the most up-to-date information as you help your employees navigate these programs. Our toolkit provides information for you and your employees.

Get Information on Job Protection
Temporary Disability Insurance and Family Leave Insurance are wage replacement programs and they do not provide job protection. However, your business or organization may be covered under the new COVID-19 anti-retaliation law, federal Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA), New Jersey Family Leave Act (NJFLA), or the NJ Security and Financial Empowerment (SAFE) Act.
Click here
for more information on job protection and retaliation.
Help Your Employees Navigate Our Website and Benefit Applications
Direct your employees to the right information and benefit application based on their individual need. Refer your employee to the appropriate page below:
Employees who are sick, injured, or post-surgery
Employees who are pregnant, or just gave birth
Employees who are bonding with a newborn baby
Employees who are bonding with a newly adopted child or newly placed foster child
Employees who are caring for an ill or injured loved one

Keep Your Employees Informed about Job Protection

However, your employee’s job may be protected under the new COVID-19 anti-retaliation law, federal Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA), New Jersey Family Leave Act (NJFLA), or the NJ Security and Financial Empowerment (SAFE) Act.
